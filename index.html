Mabinogi Wine Calculator

AcidityAcidity decreases if the player takes too long in the oak barrel replacement.
PurityPurity decreases if too many sediments are not taken out in the oak barrel replacement.
FreshnessFreshness decreases if the player neglects to replace the oak barrel after 24 real-time hours, decaying by 1 point every 36 minutes after the 24 hour mark.
AgeAge slowly increases over time at a rate of approximately 1 age every 2.8 in-game days (or 1 hour, 40 minutes and 48 seconds real-time), and reaches maximum after exactly 280 in-game days (or one real-time week).

Score = (Purity+Acidity+Freshness+Age) * (lowest value between Purity, Acidity, Freshness, and Age) * 5
